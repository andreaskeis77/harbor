# Harbor Functional Requirements v0.1

## Minimum functional requirements

1. Harbor shall support clearly separated projects.
2. Harbor shall support a versioned research handbook per project.
3. Harbor shall support project-specific source assignment.
4. Harbor shall support a reviewable project state.
5. Harbor shall support a resumable project state.
6. Harbor shall support explicit archive and blueprint workflows.
7. Harbor shall support refresh as a distinct operation from initial search.
