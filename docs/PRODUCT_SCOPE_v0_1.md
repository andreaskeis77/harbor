# Harbor Product Scope v0.1

Harbor is a project-partitioned research and monitoring system.

## Core v1 scope

- project creation and separation
- versioned research handbook
- project-local source management
- review and resume capability
- controlled refresh
- blueprint-based reuse of archived projects
- one canonical backend for website and Custom GPT

## Deliberately deferred

- broad autonomous agents
- multi-user roles
- unrestricted crawling of protected sources
- hidden cross-project coupling
