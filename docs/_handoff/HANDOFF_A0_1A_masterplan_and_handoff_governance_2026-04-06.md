## What this changes

Harbor now has an explicit three-layer continuity model:

1. long-range roadmap in `MASTERPLAN.md`
2. current accepted truth in `PROJECT_STATE.md`
3. step-level transfer notes in `docs/_handoff/`

## Why this matters

Without this layer, Harbor risks losing:
- roadmap continuity
- phase order
- the distinction between drafted vs validated baseline
- reliable handoff behavior between chats
